REAL ESTATE LEGAL KNOWLEDGE BASE

1. PROPERTY OWNERSHIP DOCUMENTS
- Sale Deed: Legal proof of ownership transfer
- Title Deed: Confirms ownership rights
- Encumbrance Certificate: Shows property has no legal dues
- Property Tax Receipts: Proof of tax payment
- Occupancy Certificate: Confirms building is legally constructed

2. RERA (REAL ESTATE REGULATION ACT)
- All builders must register projects under RERA
- Buyers can verify project details online
- Protects buyers from fraud and delays
- Ensures transparency in property transactions

3. HOME LOAN ELIGIBILITY
- Based on income, credit score, and existing loans
- EMI should not exceed 40-50% of income
- Banks require KYC documents and income proof

4. IMPORTANT LEGAL CHECKS BEFORE BUYING
- Verify clear title ownership
- Check land use approval (residential/commercial)
- Ensure no pending legal disputes
- Confirm builder approvals

5. REGISTRATION PROCESS
- Property must be registered with local authority
- Stamp duty must be paid
- Buyer and seller must sign registration documents

6. RISKS IN PROPERTY PURCHASE
- Fake ownership documents
- Illegal construction
- Delayed possession
- Hidden charges

7. PROPERTY TAX AND CHARGES
- Annual property tax must be paid
- Maintenance charges in apartments
- Utility charges (water, electricity)

8. AGREEMENT TO SELL
- Preliminary contract before final sale
- Includes terms, price, and payment schedule

9. NRI PROPERTY RULES
- NRIs can buy residential property
- Cannot buy agricultural land (in most cases)
